Duibai Privacy Policy

Version 0.1 (Draft) ｜ Effective Date 2026-07-18 ｜ Last Updated 2026-07-08 ｜ Status Draft

This policy describes how Duibai collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data, and your rights. For questions: folio0427@gmail.com.

01　Scope & Legal Basis

  • Applies to the Duibai app and related services
  • Primary: Taiwan Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)
  • EU users: GDPR
  • United States users: the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA / CPRA) and other US states with comprehensive privacy laws. Given our current scale, we may not meet the "covered business" thresholds of these laws, but we voluntarily extend the rights described below to residents of US states (see §07, "US State Privacy Rights")
  • App Store / Google Play privacy requirements are also observed

02　Data We Collect

Provided at registration

  • Google / Apple OAuth identifier (uid + email)
  • Nickname
  • Gender
  • Date of birth (for 18+ verification + age display)
  • Mode (Pure Film Friend / Open to Connection / Open)
  • Desired gender (filled in only when your mode is "Open to Connection" or "Open")
  • Favorite media types and genres (films / TV series, preferred genres; multiple choice)
  • Quick Q&A answers (optional)
  • Region (optional free text you provide; may be left blank and edited at any time)

Generated through usage

  • Post content (film / TV series, opening line, the question you want to ask)
  • Application content (your answers to a post's questions)
  • Chatroom messages (text messages in chatrooms)
  • Film-library content (films / TV series you save, star ratings, reflections, watch dates)
  • Social connections (your block list)
  • Report and block data: when you report a post or a user, we record the target of your report, the reasons you select, and the explanation you provide, and the server captures a content snapshot at the time of the report as evidence for moderation and legal defense. Where the report targets a user, that snapshot may include the reported user's personal data (nickname, gender, date of birth, mode), their recent posts, and the messages exchanged between you and that user in a shared chatroom. When you block a user, we record only the "blocker–blocked" relationship; the blocked user receives no notification. The foregoing report and block data is accessible only to Duibai's moderation / administration personnel, and only to the extent necessary.
  • Device info: OS version, app version, push token (used to receive notifications)
  • Activity logs (login time; basic records needed for server-side security and anomaly detection)

Not collected

  • Real-life photos (real-photo upload is not enabled)
  • Bank account or credit card information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts
  • Phone number
  • Microphone, camera (except future features that you actively authorize)
  • Third-party usage-analytics / behavioral-tracking data: Duibai does not use any third-party analytics SDK (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.), does not collect device-level analytics identifiers, and does not infer your coarse location

Sensitive personal data (note)

  • Your "mode" (especially "Open to Connection") together with your "desired gender" may, when read together, reveal sensitive signals such as your sexual orientation / romantic orientation. Under the California CCPA / CPRA and some US state laws, such data is treated as "sensitive personal information."
  • We use such data to provide the matching service: your "mode" drives the matching filter matrix (determining who can see whom); your "desired gender" is only a preference signal shown to eligible peers and is not itself used to filter whom you see. Such data is not sold, not used for advertising, and not used to build advertising / marketing profiles (profiling).
  • You provide such data voluntarily (opt-in) at registration; you may change your mode / desired gender at any time on the "Profile" tab (each subject to a cooldown period).

03　Purposes

  • User matching and the matchmaking algorithm
  • 18+ age verification
  • Fraud and abuse detection
  • Customer service
  • Legal-compliance obligations
  • Service push notifications (e.g., new messages, application notices, chatroom status changes) are notices necessary to provide the service. Duibai currently does not offer marketing push notifications

Service providers (data processors)

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google LLC (push notifications) — global edge
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) by Apple Inc. (iOS push) — global edge

Your search keywords are sent to TMDB to retrieve search results; the platform does not send your account identifier, personal data, or other user information to TMDB. This product uses TMDB and the TMDB APIs but is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by TMDB.
  • Letterboxd: This is an opt-in feature. If you choose to enter your Letterboxd username to import your watch history, that username is sent to Letterboxd's public RSS endpoint at letterboxd.com (`letterboxd.com/<your username>/rss/`) to retrieve that account's public viewing diary (titles, ratings, reviews, etc.). The platform does not send your Duibai account identifier, email, or other personal data to Letterboxd

Legal disclosure

  • Court orders, prosecutorial / police investigations, and lawful requests from administrative authorities
  • Imminent life-safety situations

Not shared with

  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing-analytics firms

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes; we embed no third-party advertising SDK and show no in-app ads.

Inter-user visibility

  • Nickname, mode badge, genre preferences, region (if you provide it)
  • Desired gender: shown conditionally on the other party's profile / post details per the filtering matrix, only when your mode is "Open to Connection" or "Open"
  • Gender and age: shown on the other party's profile and in post details per the mode filtering matrix (gender is not shown to Pure-Film-Friend viewers; age is derived from your date of birth, and your full date of birth is not shown). You can enable hide toggles on the "Profile" tab so that your gender / age / region / film library are not shown to other users
  • Posts, application content, and film-library reflections you publish
  • Chatroom messages after a match is made (one-on-one chatrooms); the other party in the chatroom can see the messages you send

06　Retention

During account lifetime

  • Full retention to provide the service

After account deletion

  • Personal data is automatically cleared immediately once you confirm "Leave Duibai" (see the "Account deletion" section below)
  • Data required by statutory obligations (tax, criminal investigation) is retained by law until the period expires

Specific retention periods

  • Violation, report, and related enforcement records (including reported content): retained for the purposes of community safety, abuse prevention, and legal defense. A report is retained for 5 years after it is closed (actioned or dismissed), then hard-deleted automatically by the system on expiry; reports not yet closed are retained until handling is complete. After you delete your account, the link between the record and your account is removed
  • Suspension hash identifier (a one-way salted SHA-256 hash of the OAuth sign-in identifier, used only to prevent re-registration evasion of a suspension): auto-deleted 5 years after suspension; removed immediately upon unbanning. See the "Suspension Notice & Appeal" section of the Terms of Service for the detailed mechanism
  • Terms-consent records (`user_tos_consents`): 5 years (legal evidence; hard-deleted automatically on expiry). Records include "the doc_type, version, time, and method of your acceptance of the ToS / Privacy Policy," "the IP address and the device / browser identifier string (user-agent) at the moment of your consent," and "a server-side salted SHA-256 hash of your email." The salt is held by the platform and cannot be held by the client; the hash is one-way and cannot be reversed to recover the email. This design is used to: (a) after you delete your account, still allow us to verify in litigation whether an email you claim is yours ever consented to the ToS / Privacy Policy on the platform, and (b) at the same time not retain the plaintext of your email. This constitutes pseudonymisation under GDPR Art. 4(5) and is lawful.
  • Terms-change notification delivery log (`tos_notification_log`): records the delivery and open status of terms-change notices across each channel (push / email / in-app banner / blocking modal) as evidence that the platform provided notice as required by law; retained for the same period as the terms-consent records, and after you delete your account the link to your account is removed (user_id set to NULL).

Chatroom messages

  • Your messages in a chatroom are retained until both you and the other party have deleted your accounts, or the chatroom itself is terminated
  • When you unilaterally delete your account, the messages are retained but the author name is replaced with "A film friend who left"

Account deletion ("Leave Duibai")

When you choose "Leave Duibai," the platform handles your data as follows:

Fully cleared

  • Your Google / Apple sign-in link (auth.users record)
  • Nickname, gender, birthdate, favorite media types and genres, Quick Q&A, region
  • Your film library (saved films / TV series, star ratings, reflections), posts you have made, and unprocessed applications
  • Push settings (push token)
  • Social-connection data (block list)

Retained anonymously (as a record for the other party in the chatroom)

  • Messages you have sent in a chatroom
  • The author name is replaced with "A film friend who left" when displayed
  • The other party can continue interacting in that chatroom after you leave, and the messages you previously sent remain in the chatroom in anonymised form

Legal basis: Chatroom conversations are jointly produced by all participating parties (joint controllership); we have a legitimate interest in retaining them for the other party's continued use. Anonymisation only removes the link to your account and displays the content as "A film friend who left"; to that extent it is no longer personally identifiable data. However, where a message's own content contains information that identifies an individual (e.g., a self-entered name, address, or social handle), it may still constitute personal data; the platform will handle such content in accordance with applicable law, a report, or your deletion request. This approach is consistent with messaging services such as LINE / WhatsApp / Discord.

Future re-login: Signing back in to Duibai with the same Google / Apple account will be treated as a brand-new user requiring fresh registration. The messages you previously sent in chatrooms still exist but are anonymised and are not linked to your new account.

Processing: takes effect immediately after you tap "Leave Duibai" and confirm; your auth account and personal data are cleared from our servers in the same operation.

Statutory retention:

  • Terms-consent records (user_tos_consents) — 5 years by law (including a salted SHA-256 hash of the email, non-reversible, hard-deleted automatically by the system on expiry)
  • Messages involved in legal disputes / fraud investigations
  • Anonymised statistical data that cannot be traced back to an individual

Sign in with Apple revocation and account deletion (Important)

Apple lets you revoke Duibai's Apple ID authorization via Settings → Apple ID → Password & Security → Sign in with Apple → Duibai → Stop Using.

Please note: this action only revokes the link between your Apple ID and Duibai; it does NOT automatically delete your Duibai account and data. Apple and Duibai are separate data controllers; revocation on Apple's side cannot assert your right to deletion against Duibai.

If you wish to fully delete your Duibai account, please do so in the app: the "Profile" tab → "Leave Duibai (Delete Account)" at the bottom of the page. If that path is unavailable, you may also send a deletion request to folio0427@gmail.com.

If you revoke Apple authorization but do not delete your account in the app: your Duibai data will continue to exist until you actively delete it in the app.

This handling is consistent with industry practice (Bumble, Tinder, Hinge, etc.); the goal is to avoid accidental data destruction caused by third-party platform actions and to ensure that your deletion intent is direct, explicit, and traceable.

07　Your Rights

Rights granted under PDPA §3

  • Inspect: know what personal data of yours the platform holds
  • Request a copy: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Supplement and correct: correct inaccurate data (gender / date of birth are user-editable, with a cooldown period after each change)
  • Stop collection, processing, and use: opt out of specific uses of your personal data
  • Delete: delete your account

GDPR (EU users) additional rights

  • Right to data portability: obtain your personal data in a structured format
  • Right to object to automated decision-making: object to purely automated decisions of the matching algorithm
  • Right to restrict processing: suspend processing without deletion
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a Data Protection Authority (DPA)

US State Privacy Rights (California CCPA / CPRA and other states with comprehensive privacy laws)

  • Right to know: the categories of personal data the platform collects, their sources, purposes, and with whom they are shared
  • Right to access / obtain a copy, right to delete, and right to correct
  • Right to opt out of sale / sharing: the platform does not sell and does not share for cross-context behavioral advertising your personal data, so no separate "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link is required
  • Right to limit the use of sensitive personal information: the platform uses sensitive personal information only to provide the service (matching), not for advertising or profiling
  • No discriminatory treatment for exercising the above rights
  • Given our current scale, we may not meet the thresholds set by each state law, but we still provide the above rights voluntarily; the response deadline and manner of exercise are as below

How to exercise

  • Email folio0427@gmail.com
  • The platform responds within 30 days of receipt (identity verification required)

Cases where the platform may decline or delay processing

  • Prohibited by law
  • Retained for a criminal investigation
  • Substantial impact on the significant interests of a third party
  • Requests that are clearly excessively frequent

  • Regular backups
  • 100% security cannot be guaranteed; in the event of a personal-data breach, we will notify you in accordance with Taiwan PDPA §12

11　Change Notifications

Multi-channel notice (industry standard, ensuring delivery)

  • L1 Push notification (FCM / APNs): received immediately by users who have push enabled
  • L2 Email (obtained via OAuth): 99% delivery, leaves evidence
  • L3 In-app banner: visible on opening the app
  • L4 Blocking modal: you must check "I have read and agree" to continue using the service

How consent takes effect

  • After the terms are updated, you will see a blocking consent modal the next time you open the app
  • You cannot continue using the service until you explicitly check "I have read and agree"
  • The modal's options are: agree and continue using / delete account
  • No buffer period — under GDPR / the PDPA, the platform must obtain valid consent before continuing to process your personal data, rather than granting a grace window afterward

Change classification

  • Non-material changes (typo fixes, section reordering): version bump, still re-obtaining consent via the L4 blocking modal
  • Material changes (liability, jurisdiction, termination conditions): all L1–L4 channels are used for notice
  • New personal-data collection / new third-party sharing / changed purposes: a mandatory modal forces re-consent; without consent, the relevant features are limited; no buffer period
