How to Delete Your Duibai Account

This page is provided in accordance with the Apple App Store Review Guidelines (account-deletion instructions must be public and viewable without logging in), and explains how a Duibai account is deleted and the scope of data handling.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Duibai app and log in to your account
  2. Tap "Profile" in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Tap "Leave Duibai (Delete Account)" at the bottom of the page
  4. Confirm deletion

After you confirm, your account and personal data will be cleared from the server immediately, and this action cannot be undone.

If you cannot log in to the app

If you are unable to log in to the app to perform the steps above (for example, you have forgotten your login method, your account is malfunctioning, etc.), please send a deletion request to folio0427@gmail.com and include:

  • Your Duibai nickname
  • The email of the Google or Apple account you used to register
  • A description of your deletion request

We will process it as soon as possible after receipt, and no later than 7 business days.

Deletion scope: data that will be fully cleared

  • Google / Apple sign-in link
  • Nickname, gender, birthdate, favorite media types and genres, Quick Q&A, region settings
  • Your film library (saved films / TV series, star ratings, reflections)
  • Posts you have published
  • Unprocessed applications
  • Push notification settings (push token)
  • Social-connection data (block list)

Deletion scope: data retained in anonymised form

  • Messages you have sent in one-on-one or group binge-watch chatrooms
  • When displayed, your nickname is replaced with "A film friend who left"; other chatroom members can still see the message content itself, but can no longer see that you were the sender

The purpose of this design: a chatroom conversation is an interaction record jointly produced by you and the other members of the chatroom. If an entire conversation disappeared because one party deleted their account, it would affect the experience of other members still using Duibai (for example, in a group binge-watch chatroom, the conversation records of the other 2–7 members would be disrupted). This is consistent with the practices of mainstream messaging services such as LINE, WhatsApp, and Discord.

If a message's content itself contains information that can identify you personally (for example, a real name or contact details you entered yourself), you may submit a separate handling request via folio0427@gmail.com.

Deletion scope: data that must be retained by law

The following data must continue to be retained due to statutory obligations, and its link to your account will be removed (not traceable back to you personally):

  • Terms-consent records: retained for 5 years (only a string derived from an irreversible hash of your email is retained, as legal evidence of "whether you ever consented to the platform's terms"; the plaintext email is not retained)
  • Data related to ongoing legal disputes or fraud investigations: retained until the investigation or proceeding concludes
  • Fully anonymised statistical data that cannot be traced back to an individual

Rejoining Duibai

After you delete your account, if you sign back in to Duibai with the same Google or Apple account, you will be treated as a brand-new user and must complete the registration process again. The anonymised messages you previously left in chatrooms will not be linked in any way to your new account.

Contact us

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please email folio0427@gmail.com.
